Whether you are an experienced gym user or a complete beginner, our team can help you achieve the results you have been looking for in our modern, comfortable, and clean Gym and fitness club. Gym Facilities

MAXFIT provides our members with the top quality cardio, free weight, and strength training equipment they need to get in shape. Our gym is stocked with world-class equipment from leading manufacturers such as Life Fitness and Hammer Strength. Our equipment and workout spaces are clean, safe, and comfortable. And our Team are always on hand to offer support and advice in the best use of our equipment. Our equipment list include; Excercise bikes, treadmils, incline bench presses, hammer strenght machines, barbells and dumbells, pullup bars, lateral pulldown machines, leg extension machines, hyper extension benches, smith machine, peck deck machines, and more!.
