'Your Club.Your Way'


MAXFIT is Gym & Fitness Club with a Passion. Our Passion is the long-term health and fitness of our Members.

At the heart of what we do is a mission to maximise the potential within each of our Club members, and to guide them along an individually-tailored path to reaching their goals. Located on the breath-taking shores of Lough Leane, 5 minutes from the heart of Killarney, our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to empower people of all ages, regardless of their fitness levels. And with 24-hour access available, we are always there when you need us.

We are a passionate and dedicated team of multi-diciplinary fitness professionals and employ best in class training methodologies, wellbeing techniques, nutritional science, and meal planning. We can ensure our members can work towards achievable and meaningful fitness goals. Whatever age, or level of fitness you are at, we can advise you on a fitness program tailored specifically for you.

Talk to us to today to start your journey to a healthier and happier you!

What We Do

Our Gym

At MAXFIT we source the best gym equipment for our members, and maintain and upgrade that equipment to maintain the highest standards of a modern Gym. Our Gym is laid out across 3 split-level floors, which are surrounded by inspirational views of the Killarney lakes helping you to stay motivated. Each floor is dedicated to one of three training diciplines, those being; Cardio machines, Upper & Lower body machines, and lifting & free weights. Out team are experts in the use of all equipment and know how to get maxmise results from their use while ensuring your safety.
